About Malaysia

Discover our exclusive offer to study English in Malaysia! In collaboration with a prestigious and well-known language center, we offer comprehensive IELTS preparation programs that will help you achieve your desired scores, along with immersive 6-month language courses. These courses are designed to improve your overall English proficiency, enabling you to communicate confidently in both academic and everyday settings. The courses start from just 2000 euros, making it an affordable option for those looking to enhance their language skills. Furthermore, after successfully completing the language courses, we provide you with a unique opportunity to further your academic journey by enrolling in some of the top universities in Malaysia or Australia. Whether you are looking to pursue a degree in Malaysia or want to experience studying in Australia, we will guide you through the entire process and ensure a smooth transition to university life. This is an excellent opportunity for those wishing to broaden their horizons and study in renowned institutions while improving their English at the same time

checklist   Documents

To apply you must provide the following documents:

  • Copy of the passport;
  • Identity Photo – A recent, clear passport-sized photograph. The photo must be a standard passport photo format (35mm x 45mm)
  • High School Graduation Diploma or its equivalent – copy and legalized translation, authenticated (show / hide) by the authorities in the issuing country;
  • High scool Transcript of Records / Diploma Supplement – copy and legalized translation
  • Medical certificate (issued in an international language) to certify that the candidate does not suffer from contagious diseases or from conditions that are incompatible with the prospective profession;
  • File processing fee receipt.

attach_money   Fees

The program duration is 6 months. The admission fee is €750 , while the study fee is €2000 for the entire duration. Accommodation costs for 6 months are €798, which includes Wi-Fi fees, but excludes electricity and water charges. Additionally, the accommodation service includes three cleaning sessions per month for common areas. There is also an administrative and contract fee of approximately €40. These costs provide a comprehensive package that covers all necessary services, allowing you to focus on your studies while enjoying a comfortable living environment.

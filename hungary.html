About Hungary

Hungary offers a high standard of education combined with a rich cultural experience, making it an attractive destination for international students. Whether you're pursuing a Bachelor's, Master's, or Doctoral degree, our services simplify the entire application process. We guide you through the necessary steps, such as school certificate recognition, entrance exams, and securing a student visa. description  Requirements

The requirements for international students to study in Hungary are straightforward.

University Admission Requirements

Entry and admission requirements in Hungary follow the standards of the Bologna Process. This means that a Bachelor’s degree must be obtained to have access to Master’s and PhD courses. To be eligible for a BA degree you must have your language proficiency certificates, your diploma and other documents required by the programme.

Bachelor Programs

To apply for a Bachelor’s degree (3 years), prospective students must provide:

  • High school diploma translated into Hungarian or English, along with the corresponding transcript of courses and grades.
  • Some programs may require an entrance exam in addition to submitting these documents.

Master Programs

For a Master’s degree (2 years) application applicants have to submit the photocopy of their Bachelor’s diploma and probably have to take an entrance exam.

Doctoral Programs

If someone wishes to obtain a PhD degree (2+2 years), the minimum requirement is to have a university degree which is equivalent to a master’s degree. The prerequisite for entering doctoral courses is an MA/MSc degree or an equivalent to an MA/MSc degree. Within a Doctoral programme, you need to complete 240 credits within 4 years of study. At the end of the fourth semester, a final examination must be taken. After a successful examination, the last 2 years of the doctoral programme are about conducting the research and writing your dissertation, which can be extended with one more year.

language   Language Requirements

Language requirements for BA and Bsc students
A B2 complex proficiency certificate in English language or a certificate of equal value IELTS 5.5 or higher, TOEFL 60 or higher if the language of instruction is other than English.
Language requirements for master and doctoral level degree programmes
Although the programmes have different language requirements, an advanced level English language knowledge is necessary to get admission to the programmes, as the language of instruction is English. Advanced level means level C1 according to the Common European Framework of Reference for Languages (CEFR). Language knowledge of students are tested through a personal or a Skype interview. Proof of language knowledge might be requested in the admission criteria of the programmes. Some programmes require a complex (written and oral) language exam at a B2 level in order to get admission. In case the student provides a language exam in a language other than English, he/she will still be obliged to prove their English language skills.

checklist   Documents

To apply for admission to universities in Hungary, students must provide the following documents:

  • Up-to-date CV
  • Letter of motivation
  • Copy of your passport/ID
  • Proof of language proficiency
  • English translation of transcripts of records
  • English translation of obtained diplomas
  • Two academic reference letters

attach_money   Fees

In Hungary, education fees vary depending on the chosen university, program, and the student's nationality. Generally, for undergraduate programs, you can expect to pay between 3,000 – 5,000 € per year, for master's programs, between 4,000 – 6,000 € per year, and for doctoral programs, between 6,000 – 8,000 € per year
